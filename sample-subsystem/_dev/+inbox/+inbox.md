# +inbox/

Local capture point for this subsystem: quick notes, stray observations, and untriaged items that
surface mid-work but do not yet belong to an effort or a domain. Read at session warm-up so nothing
sits here unseen, then triaged on the next pass into an effort task, a domain note, or the archive.

Distinct from a domain's parked-decisions section (deliberate deferrals, each with a re-open
trigger) and from the LOG (material changes already made). The inbox is untriaged by definition.

This sample keeps the inbox empty to show the shape. In real use, entries are short and
short-lived: capture, triage, clear.
